ABOUT US

Fitness club and its professional trainers. Within the walls of this private fitness facility, the story of Liam Rushmer’s passion and determination to provide the best to his clients is truly understood. Fitness Rush was created by owner and founder Liam Rushmer more than 10 years ago from his parents’ garage where he learned the art of bodybuilding through nutrition and exercise. Between training family and friends to build upon his knowledge and trading gym equipment, the first business named Just in gyms was born. After 2 years of training, the current home of the gym was acquired for the expansion of Just in gyms which succeeded for a further 2 years before the decision was made to share the benefits of the expansive range of equipment within the local community.
